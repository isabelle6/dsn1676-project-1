Blue Tang

The Blue Tang is a species of surgeonfish. They have a royal blue body with a black design, and a tail that is yellow. They are very flat fish and grow up to 30 centimetres (about 11.8 inches) in length. They live in pairs or in groups of up to 10 or 12 fish.

Source: Wikipedia

Clownfish

Clownfish are normally orange in colour, but can also be yellow, red, or black, depending on the species, and many of them have white bars or patches. The largest kind can be up to 18 centimetres (about 7.1 inches) in length, while the smallest can be 10 centimetres (about 3.9 inches) long.

Source: Wikipedia

Starfish

Starfish are star shaped marine invertebrates. There are about 1,500 species of starfish on the seabed in oceans all over the world, from the tropics to frigid polar waters. They typically have five arms, although some species have more.

Source: Wikipedia
